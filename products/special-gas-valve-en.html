Special control valve

Purpose (application scope):Chemical industry, medical industry

Size range (diameter), mm:25–80

Pressure range, MPa:0.5–3.0

Design features:Irrespective of pressure value, the gas feed accuracy is up to 0.1%

Preferable alternatives:Conventional and ball cocks

Embodiment featuresall items and components are either made of corrosion-resistant materials

Small-size control valves (25 to 80 mm in diameter) are intended for high-precision dosage of gaseous fluids. Accurate control of fluid flow rate passing through the control valve allows qualitative modifications to process procedures of diverse industrial and medical plants.

Successive installation of several control valves does not affect accuracy of final result.

We offer various versions of control valves differing by material they are made of (plastic, non-ferrous metals, their combinations), and by control systems with preset quantitative flow control accuracy classes given different initial parameters.

Unlike other types of regulation, we offer accurate determination of flow behavior by pre-adjustment of its parameter instead of post-evaluation against additional and indirect parameters.
