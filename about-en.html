About us

Igor Nikolaevich Karelin
doctor of Engineering Science, Professor RSU of Oil and Gas after I.M. Gubkin

For the first time, the innovative control valve design was presented by I.N. Karelin, professor RSU of Oil and Gas after I.M. Gubkin, in 1999.

The idea was polished up after obtaining the simulation results, although even the initial design demonstrated its significant advantages compared to analogous equipment which is widely marketed and conventionally used in oil and gas industry

In 2016, we fabricated a pilot specimen which underwent mechanical tests based on which results we gave preference to the passage design in the form of 6-ray star rather than 8-ray that. Thereby, we managed to reduce friction.

In March 2017, we fabricated another pilot specimen ready for field testing.

In March 2018, Test Center 'Pricam Test' at JSC 'Votkinsky Zavod' ran the casing tightness tests.

In March 2019, patents for invention № 2681726 and № 2682468 were issued.

In 2019, Canada National Patent for invention № CA 2999365 was issued.

As of June 23 2020 Standing Committee on Introduction of Innovative Solution to PJSC 'Gazprom' decided to include the Regulator «STAR» in Register of Innovative Solutions for Introduction to PJSC 'Gazprom'. Identification number in the Register 1.13.3.1.
